Our Story

Born from a passion for the perfect sear, our steakhouse is the soul of the hotel. We believe that great dining starts with the finest hand-selected cuts and the ancient power of an open flame. Every steak we serve is aged to perfection and grilled with precision to honor the natural flavors of the meat. More than just a meal, we offer a sanctuary for those who appreciate culinary craft and refined atmosphere. Whether it’s a quiet dinner for two or a grand celebration, our table is your home. From our signature sauces to our world-class wine cellar, every detail is curated for excellence. We don't just cook; we celebrate the legacy of the classic steakhouse. Savor the warmth of our hospitality and the intensity of our grill. This is where tradition meets luxury, one plate at a time.
